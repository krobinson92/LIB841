Meet Kate

The Professional

My name is Kate Robinson, and I am a first-year School Library Media Specialist at Shelby County West Middle School. For 9 years, I have been an English/Language Arts teacher to students in grades 6-10, and I am excited to begin my new journey.

I have a B.S. Middle/Secondary Education (English/Language Arts), M.Ed. Teacher Leadership, and I am currently completing a Certification in School Library.

The Wife and Mother

I have been married to my husband for 6 years, and we have two beautiful daughters together. In our spare time, we enjoy spending time outdoors. When the weather is nice, we love to walk our dog, Ruby, watch the plants grow in our garden, and play silly games.
